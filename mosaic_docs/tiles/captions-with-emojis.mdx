Automatically generate stylized captions for your video that include relevant emojis. This helps make short-form content more engaging, expressive, and attention-grabbing—especially on platforms like TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

## How It Works

This tile:

- Transcribes speech from your video
- Adds readable captions timed to the audio
- Inserts emojis based on meaning, tone, and keywords
- Formats captions for vertical viewing

![Captions with Emojis](/images/captions-emojis-ui.png)

No configuration is required — just add and run.
