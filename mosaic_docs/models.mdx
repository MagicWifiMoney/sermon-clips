description: "AI models available across Mosaic features, billed by generation seconds."

Mosaic uses a range of AI models across its creation tiles. Each model is billed by **generation seconds** — the duration of the content it produces. One subscription gives you access to all models.

---

## <Icon icon="wand-sparkles" /> AI Augment

Apply targeted AI visual transformations to short segments of your video. [Learn more](/tiles/ai-augment)

| Model | Unit |
|-------|------|
| AI Augment | Generation Seconds |
