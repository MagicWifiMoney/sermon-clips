All subscription and billing management happens in one place — **Settings > Plans & Billing** inside the Mosaic editor.

<Card title="Open Plans & Billing" icon="gear" href="https://edit.mosaic.so/?settings_org=7cab1f8a-8161-4619-a36d-3dd3709dd7c0&settings_tab=billing">
  Go to Settings > Plans & Billing in the Mosaic editor
</Card>

---

## What You Can Do

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="View your current plan" icon="eye">
  See which plan you're on, what's included, and when your next billing cycle is. Head to **Settings > Plans & Billing** to view your plan details at any time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Upgrade or change your plan" icon="arrow-up-right">
  Ready for more? Click **Manage Billing** inside Plans & Billing to upgrade to a higher tier or switch between monthly and annual billing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Update payment methods" icon="credit-card">
  Need to swap your card or update billing details? Click **Manage Billing** to securely update your payment information.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cancel your subscription" icon="xmark">
  You can cancel anytime from **Manage Billing**. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

---

## Quick Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the settings icon in the Mosaic editor, or use the link above.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Plans & Billing">
    Select the **Plans & Billing** tab to see your current plan and usage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Manage Billing">
    To change your plan, update payment methods, or cancel — click **Manage Billing** to open the billing portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
