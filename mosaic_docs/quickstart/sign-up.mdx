Getting started takes less than a minute:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to edit.mosaic.so">
    Open [**edit.mosaic.so**](https://edit.mosaic.so) in your browser — no downloads or installs needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up using Google or email, then verify your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on your Canvas">
    You'll arrive at your **Mosaic Canvas** — your visual workspace where all editing happens.
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's it — no installers, no plugins, no messy setup. You're ready to build your first workflow.
