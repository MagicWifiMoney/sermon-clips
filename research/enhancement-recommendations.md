## Critical Pain Points Mapped to Solutions

### Pain Point 1: "How can you possibly keep this schedule up without it controlling your life?"

**Current Reality:**
- 1.5-2 hours editing per sermon
- Weekly clips, captions, thumbnails across IG, FB, TikTok, YouTube
- Burnout is a major concern
- One-person teams are common

**Mosaic-Powered Solution:**
- **YouTube Triggers**: Set up once, automatically processes every new sermon upload
- **Destination Tile**: One-click publishing to all 6 platforms simultaneously
- **Webhook Integration**: Real-time notifications when clips are ready
- Reduces weekly workload from 4+ hours to 10 minutes (as testimonial states)

### Pain Point 2: "Sermon engagement throughout the week" (now a tracked metric)

**Current Reality:**
- Churches need to measure sermon impact beyond Sunday
- Digital engagement is a key 2026 metric
- Current MVP doesn't address mid-week engagement

**Mosaic-Powered Solution:**
- **Scheduled Publishing**: Drip-feed clips throughout the week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday)
- **Platform-Specific Optimization**: Different clips for different audiences/platforms
- **AI Caption Variations**: Tailored messaging for each day/platform
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track which clips drive engagement when

### Pain Point 3: "The footage keeps piling and I am overwhelmed"

**Current Reality:**
- Backlog of unedited footage
- Can't justify new tools when appearing to fail with current ones
- Progress hindered by volume

**Mosaic-Powered Solution:**
- **Batch Processing**: Upload multiple sermons, process simultaneously
- **Automated Workflows**: No manual editing required
- **Silence Removal**: Automatically clean up dead air
- **Template-Based Processing**: Consistent output without manual intervention

### Pain Point 4: Platform chaos - "TikTok wants vertical. YouTube wants landscape."

**Current Reality:**
- Need different formats for different platforms
- Manual reformatting is time-consuming
- Inconsistent quality across platforms

**Mosaic-Powered Solution:**
- **Reframe Tile**: Automatically generates all aspect ratios from single source
- **Platform-Specific Optimization**: Each platform gets ideal format
- **Consistent Branding**: Watermark, intro/outro applied to all formats
- **Cinematic Captions**: Professional styling for each platform

### Pain Point 5: "We cannot do this alone... but we don't have many folks"

**Current Reality:**
- Small churches have limited volunteer resources
- Can't hire professional editors
- Need solutions that work for one-person teams

**Mosaic-Powered Solution:**
- **Fully Automated Pipeline**: From upload to publishing
- **No Technical Skills Required**: Set up once, runs automatically
- **Volunteer-Friendly**: Anyone can monitor, no editing expertise needed
- **Scalable**: Works for 1 person or 10-person team

### Pain Point 6: "Too much promotion causes follower loss"

**Current Reality:**
- Need content that feels valuable, not just promotional
- Struggle to balance ministry message with engagement
- "I love your posts because they're always ___"

**Mosaic-Powered Solution:**
- **AI Caption Generation**: Customizable prompts for different content types
  - "Make it encouraging and add scripture references"
  - "Write for Instagram with a strong hook about overcoming challenges"
  - "Create a thought-provoking question for LinkedIn"
- **Quote Graphics**: Extract powerful moments as standalone graphics
- **Audiograms**: Audio-only snippets for different consumption contexts
- **Variety**: Mix of video clips, quotes, audiograms keeps feed fresh
